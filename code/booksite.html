Exploring Fantasy Books

In this website we will explore the history of the genre of fantasy in books, as well as look at some noteable works from the genre.

What is fantasy?

Fantasy is a genre usually set in a fictional universe, often involving magic and different types of creatures. Some rather popular types of creatures include dragons, dwarves and elves. Some inspirations for this genre include mythology and fairy tales.
